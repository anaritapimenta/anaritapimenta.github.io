Project Overview

At Livemotion, I wrote newsletters distributed through leading marketing magazines and created banner copy for those platforms. My work included crafting high-performing media campaigns tailored to their audience.

I focused on campaign results:

  • How many views did each banner generate?
  • What was the click-through rate from ads to the website?
  • How well did each newsletter perform?

I managed the operational and strategic aspects of media performance, understanding how each magazine contributed to our broader communication goals.
