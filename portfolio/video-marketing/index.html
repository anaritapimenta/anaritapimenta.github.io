Project Details: Video Marketing

Admittedly not my main area of expertise, I explored the emotional power of video marketing in this project.

Video is one of the most powerful and emotional tools in marketing, and this project for MAR Kayaks reflected that through corporate and promotional videos.

I created several videos for MAR Kayaks, Lda, including corporate campaigns for Christmas and April Fools’, alongside promotional videos for app launch and rowing boat showcases.
