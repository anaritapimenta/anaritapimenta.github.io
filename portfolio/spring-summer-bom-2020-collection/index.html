Campaign Overview

I contributed to the strategic definition of communication channels to better engage Barn of Monkeys’ audience for the Spring/Summer 2020 season.

This included outlining a comprehensive marketing and communication plan and working closely with designers and videographers to shape the campaign’s concept and visual identity.

I also created product copy and storytelling pieces to give each item in the collection its own narrative — making the campaign more meaningful and memorable.
