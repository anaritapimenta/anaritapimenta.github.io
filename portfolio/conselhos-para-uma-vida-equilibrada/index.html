Conselhos para uma Vida Equilibrada

After listening to the client, it became clear that the message should not be commercial but informative. After all, the mission of the company is to help people feel better and live a healthier life.

I completely agreed. So, I proposed using humour to present the organisation's services in a more engaging and appealing way.

The result was a series of thought-provoking phrases. “Conselhos para uma Vida Equilibrada” became both a regular column and a clever pun on the company's name, "Centro Terapêutico Equilibrium".
