Creative Strategy & Tone

After consulting with the client, we agreed that the messaging should be informative, not commercial. The goal: help people live healthier, more balanced lives.

To make the content engaging, I proposed using humor — a tone that both reflected the brand’s personality and connected emotionally with the audience.

The result was a playful and informative series of posts under the name “Conselhos para uma Vida Equilibrada”, a smart reference to the company’s name, Centro Terapêutico Equilibrium.
