Conselhos para uma Vida Equilibrada

After listening to the client, it became clear that the message should not be commercial but informative. After all, the mission of the company is to help people to feel better and to have a healthy life.

I couldn't agree more with this statement. So, I proposed using humor to share the services of the organization in a more interesting and appealing way.

The result was to write some phrases that could stop the reader a bit. "Conselhos para uma Vida Equilibrada" is a kind of a rubric and a pun of "Centro Terapêutico Equilibrium", that is the name of the company.
