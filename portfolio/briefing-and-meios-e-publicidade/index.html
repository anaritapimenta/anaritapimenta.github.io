Project Description

At Livemotion, I wrote newsletters distributed through key marketing magazines and crafted copy for banner ads in those same publications. But creating content is just one part of the job — it's equally crucial to measure results.

I continuously asked:

  • How many views did our banners receive?
  • How many clicks to the website came from each banner?
  • What was the open rate of each newsletter?

I was responsible for the operational side of the media strategy, while also understanding the value and strategic role of each magazine in our communication efforts.
