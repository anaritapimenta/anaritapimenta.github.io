Briefing and Meios & Publicidade

At Livemotion, I wrote newsletters that were distributed through key marketing magazines and crafted the copy for banner ads placed in those same publications. However, creating content is just one part of the job — it is just as crucial to measure the exact outcome of each communication effort.

I continuously asked:

  • How many views did our banners receive?
  • How many clicks to the website came from a specific banner?
  • What was the open rate of each newsletter?

I was not only responsible for the operational side of the media strategy but also understood the value and strategic role of each magazine in our overall communication ecosystem.
