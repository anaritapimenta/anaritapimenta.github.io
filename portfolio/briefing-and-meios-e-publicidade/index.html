Campaign Goals & Impact

At Livemotion, I created newsletters for distribution in leading marketing magazines and wrote copy for banners featured in those same publications.

Beyond content creation, I focused on measuring performance:

  • How many views did each banner get?
  • What was the click-through rate?
  • Which newsletters had the highest open rates?

I managed both the hands-on execution and strategic evaluation, ensuring each media outlet contributed effectively to our brand goals.
