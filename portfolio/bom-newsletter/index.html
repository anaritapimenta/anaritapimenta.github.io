Project Overview

Every Wednesday, I wrote and curated a newsletter for Barn of Monkeys, strategically scheduled for optimal open rates based on past performance data.

The tone and structure varied depending on the objective. Some editions were designed to be corporate and institutional, while others promoted seasonal sales or product launches.
