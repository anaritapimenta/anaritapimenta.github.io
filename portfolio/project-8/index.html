EDP Running Wonders and Portugal a Correr - Events

Aveiro, Portimão, Vila do Conde, Viseu, Porto, Évora, Coimbra were some of the cities where Portugal a Correr and EDP Running Wonders passed on.

I worked as a PR specialist and I wrote several press releases, and scripts to the spokesperson.

However, on the day of the event I gave all the support, not only to journalists, videographers, and photographers, but to the general public: answering their questions, making athletes' subscriptions at the event, coordinating in the delivery of prizes.
