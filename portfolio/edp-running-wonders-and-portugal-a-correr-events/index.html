EDP Running Wonders & Portugal a Correr – Events

Aveiro, Portimão, Vila do Conde, Viseu, Porto, Évora, and Coimbra were some of the cities where Portugal a Correr and EDP Running Wonders took place.

I worked as a PR specialist and wrote several press releases and scripts for the spokesperson.

On the day of the event, I provided support not only to journalists, videographers, and photographers, but also to the general public: answering their questions, managing athlete registrations on-site, and coordinating prize presentations.
