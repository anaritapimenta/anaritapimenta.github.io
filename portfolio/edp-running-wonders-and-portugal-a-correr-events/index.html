Public Relations & Media Handling

From Aveiro to Évora, I supported EDP Running Wonders and Portugal a Correr in multiple cities across Portugal — including Porto, Portimão, Vila do Conde, Viseu, and Coimbra.

As a PR and event coordinator, I wrote press releases, spokesperson scripts, and coordinated logistics with media outlets on-site.

I also provided direct support during race days, including assisting journalists and athletes, managing live registrations, and organizing prize ceremonies.
